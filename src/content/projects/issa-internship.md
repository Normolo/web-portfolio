## Overview

During the summer between my first and second year at Junior College, I interned at the **Institute of Space Sciences and Astronomy (ISSA)** to gain hands-on experience in space sciences.

I worked with Mr Connor Sant Fournier and Prof. Jackson Said, starting with foundational Python tasks such as plotting data, running calculations, and analyzing simple datasets.

## Key work completed

- Built confidence with Python for scientific data analysis
- Developed increasingly complex analysis scripts as the internship progressed
- Created a basic satellite-orbit simulation around a planet
- Analyzed NASA exoplanet data and built a comparison tool for planetary properties

## Outcome

This internship strengthened my programming and mathematical skills, and confirmed my interest in exoplanet research and a long-term career in space sciences.
